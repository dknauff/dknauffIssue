About

A practicing software developer and experienced music educator in the Indianapolis area. Growing up I found my first passion in music and devoted much of my time to improving my knowledge and skills as a musician. Then, through my time at Butler University, I earned my Bachelor’s in music education and further honed my craft. I am currently studying at the Eleven Fifty Academy, where I am refining my skills as a software developer. I plan to use my expertise to support a company or organization through a software development position upon the completion of the academy.
